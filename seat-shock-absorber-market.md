<p><p>The Seat Shock Absorber market primarily comprises two types: spring and rubber shock absorbers. Spring shock absorbers utilize coiled metal springs to absorb and dissipate energy, providing a smooth ride by minimizing the impact of bumps and vibrations. In contrast, rubber shock absorbers rely on elastomeric materials, which offer flexibility and resilience, effectively dampening shock and vibration. Both types serve to enhance passenger comfort and safety in vehicles by improving ride quality and stability on various terrains.</p></p>
