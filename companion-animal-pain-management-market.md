<p><p>The Companion Animal Pain Management market is categorized into two primary types: external use and internal use. External use products, such as topical pain relief creams, gels, or patches, are applied directly to the affected area, providing localized relief and reducing the risk of systemic side effects. Internal use products, including oral medications, injections, or infusions, target pain through systemic absorption, aiming for overall pain control and management within the animal's body. Both types are essential for ensuring the well-being of companion animals.</p></p>
