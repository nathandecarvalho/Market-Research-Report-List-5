<p><p>The livestock pain management market is categorized into two primary types: external use and internal use. External use products include topical analgesics, creams, and patches that alleviate pain through application on the skin. These are commonly used for injuries or surgeries. Internal use products consist of oral medications or injections that target pain relief from within the animal's body, addressing conditions like arthritis or postoperative pain. Both types aim to enhance animal welfare and optimize productivity in livestock management.</p></p>
