<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Standalone Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standalone ophthalmic ultrasound systems encompass various types tailored for different diagnostic purposes. A-Scan measures axial length of the eye, aiding in intraocular lens calculations. B-Scan provides cross-sectional imaging, helpful for assessing retinal conditions and tumors. Combined Scan integrates both A- and B-Scan functionalities for comprehensive evaluations. Pachymeters measure corneal thickness, essential for glaucoma assessments. Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM) offers high-resolution imaging of the anterior segment structures, assisting in detailed ocular examinations. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing ophthalmic diagnostics.</p></p>
