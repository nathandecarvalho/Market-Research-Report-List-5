<p><p>The electronic copper foil market comprises two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through a physical rolling process, providing a thinner and more uniform product, ideal for high-density applications in electronics. Electrolytic copper foil, created via an electrochemical deposition process, offers superior conductivity and is often used in PCB manufacturing and batteries. Both types serve critical roles in the electronics industry, supporting advancements in technology and miniaturization.</p></p>
