<p><p>Commercial vacuum cleaners are categorized into three main types: upright, canister, and cylinder. Upright vacuums are designed for efficiency, featuring a vertical orientation for easy maneuverability and powerful suction, ideal for carpets and larger areas. Canister vacuums consist of a separate body and hose, offering versatility for cleaning various surfaces and hard-to-reach spots. Cylinder vacuums, typically compact and lightweight, efficiently navigate tight spaces, making them suitable for offices and smaller commercial environments. Each type caters to specific cleaning needs and preferences.</p></p>
