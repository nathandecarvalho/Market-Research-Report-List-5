<p><p>The Diagnostics PCR market comprises three main types: Real-Time PCR, Digital PCR, and Other PCR methods. Real-Time PCR, also known as quantitative PCR, enables the simultaneous amplification and quantification of DNA, providing rapid results. Digital PCR offers even greater precision, partitioning samples to assess nucleic acid amounts more accurately, often used for rare mutation detection. Other PCR methods may include traditional endpoint PCR and multiplex PCR, serving various diagnostic applications, including infectious disease detection, genetic testing, and oncology.</p></p>
