<p><p>The Aircraft Carbon Braking System market can be categorized into two types: Pneumatical Type and Hydraulical Type. Pneumatical Type braking systems use compressed air to actuate the brakes, providing precise control and reliability. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different requirements in the aircraft industry, ensuring safety and performance during landings and deceleration.</p></p>
