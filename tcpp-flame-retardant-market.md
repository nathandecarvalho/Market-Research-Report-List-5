<p><strong>The TCPP Flame Retardant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endothermic Degradation</li><li>Dilution of Gas Phase</li><li>Gas Phase Radical Quenching</li><li>Thermal Shielding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TCPP flame retardant market operates through various mechanisms: **Endothermic Degradation** absorbs heat, reducing material temperature. **Dilution of Gas Phase** lowers flammable gas concentration by releasing non-flammable gases. **Gas Phase Radical Quenching** interrupts combustion by neutralizing free radicals. **Thermal Shielding** forms a protective barrier, preventing heat transfer. These methods enhance fire safety in materials like plastics, foams, and coatings, catering to industries such as construction, electronics, and automotive, ensuring compliance with safety standards and reducing fire hazards effectively.</p></p>
