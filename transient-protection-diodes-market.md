<p><p>The Transient Protection Diodes market is divided into two types: uni-polar and bi-polar transient protection diodes. Uni-polar transient diodes are designed to protect circuits from voltage spikes in one direction, making them suitable for applications requiring unidirectional protection. In contrast, bi-polar transient diodes can handle voltage spikes in both directions, offering enhanced protection for alternating current circuits. These distinctions cater to different application needs, influencing the choice of diode based on the specific electrical environment and protection level required.</p></p>
