<p><strong>The Digital Cine Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prime Lenses</li><li>Zoom Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital cine lenses market can be categorized into prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses offer fixed focal lengths, delivering superior image quality, shallow depth of field, and exceptional low-light performance, making them ideal for cinematic storytelling. In contrast, zoom lenses provide versatile focal length options within a single lens, allowing filmmakers to adapt quickly to varying shooting scenarios without changing lenses. Both types cater to different creative needs, enhancing the visual narrative in filmmaking.</p></p>
