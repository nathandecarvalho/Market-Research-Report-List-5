<p><p>The K-12 education learning management systems (LMS) market is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based LMS are hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, offering scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT requirements, making them cost-effective for many schools. In contrast, on-premise LMS are installed locally on school servers, providing greater control over data and customizability but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different institutional needs and preferences.</p></p>
