<p><p>The foam pouch market is categorized into four types based on polyethylene density. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers strength and rigidity, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) strikes a balance between flexibility and sturdiness, often used for moderate strength needs. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and lightweight, ideal for lightweight packaging. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines the benefits of LDPE with enhanced tensile strength, making it versatile for various packaging requirements.</p></p>
