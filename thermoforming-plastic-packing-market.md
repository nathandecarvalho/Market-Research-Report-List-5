<p><p>The thermoforming plastic packing market consists of three primary types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanical formed packaging. Vacuum forming involves heating plastic sheets and forming them over a mold using suction, creating lightweight and versatile products. Pressure forming uses air pressure to achieve more detailed and precise shapes, enhancing aesthetics and functionality. Mechanical forming employs mechanical pressure to shape plastic, offering a strong and durable option for packaging. Each method serves distinct applications based on design and material needs.</p></p>
