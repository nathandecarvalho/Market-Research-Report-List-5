<p><p>The Polyether Ether Ketone (PEEK) interbody device market is segmented into various types based on surgical approaches. Posterolateral fusion devices are used for fusions involving the back muscles. Interbody fusion devices are placed between the vertebrae for structural support. Anterior lumbar interbody fusion devices (ALIF) are approached from the front, while extreme lateral interbody fusion devices (XLIF) allow for lateral access. Posterior lumbar interbody fusion devices (PLIF) and transforaminal lumbar interbody fusion devices (TLIF) access the spine from the back or side, respectively. Other devices include specialized or emerging technologies in spinal fusion.</p></p>
