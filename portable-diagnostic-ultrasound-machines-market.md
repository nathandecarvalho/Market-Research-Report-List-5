<p><strong>The Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-mode</li><li>B-mode</li><li>C-mode</li><li>F-mode</li><li>M-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable diagnostic ultrasound machines market encompasses various modes, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. A-mode (Amplitude mode) displays one-dimensional data using spikes corresponding to echo strength. B-mode (Brightness mode) generates two-dimensional images for detailed anatomical visualization. C-mode (Capacitance mode) is used in specialized applications, primarily for tissue characterization. F-mode (Frequency mode) focuses on the Doppler effect to assess blood flow. M-mode (Motion mode) captures movement over time, commonly used in cardiac assessments. Each mode enhances the versatility of portable ultrasound technology.</p></p>
