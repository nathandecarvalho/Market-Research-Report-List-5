<p><p>The Portable Enteral Feeding Pump market is primarily categorized into Continuous Feeding Type and Cycled Feeding Type. Continuous Feeding Pumps deliver formula consistently over a 24-hour period, ensuring steady nutrient intake for patients requiring prolonged support. In contrast, Cycled Feeding Pumps allow for feeding during specific hours, typically during the day or night, facilitating greater patient mobility and comfort. Both types cater to different patient needs, emphasizing ease of use, portability, and adaptability in enteral nutrition management.</p></p>
