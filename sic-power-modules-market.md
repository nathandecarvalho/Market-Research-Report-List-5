<p><p>SiC power modules are essential components in modern power electronics, categorized mainly into Hybrid SiC Modules and Full SiC Modules. Hybrid SiC Modules combine silicon and silicon carbide devices, offering a balance between performance and cost, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, Full SiC Modules utilize entirely silicon carbide technology, delivering superior efficiency, thermal performance, and high-temperature operation, making them ideal for high-power applications like electric vehicles and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
