<p><p>The containerboard market primarily consists of two main types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard is a heavy paperboard used for the outer layers of shipping containers, providing strength and protection. Corrugating medium, on the other hand, is a fluted, wavy paper that forms the inner layer, creating cushioning and structural integrity. Together, these components are essential for producing corrugated boxes, which are vital for packaging, shipping, and storage across various industries, contributing significantly to the global packaging sector.</p></p>
