<p><ul><li>Closed</li><li>Open</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil dry cleaning machine market is divided into two primary types: closed and open systems. Closed systems involve a sealed environment where solvent is recycled, minimizing exposure and reducing environmental impact. These machines are more efficient and safer for operators. In contrast, open systems expose the solvent to the atmosphere, making them simpler and less expensive but potentially more hazardous due to emissions. The choice between these types depends on operational needs, regulatory requirements, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
