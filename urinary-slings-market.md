<p><p>The urinary slings market is divided into two primary types: vaginal slings and male slings. Vaginal slings, designed for women, are used to treat urinary incontinence by providing support to the bladder and urethra through a mesh material placed under the pelvic floor. Male slings, tailored for men, often address urinary incontinence following prostate surgery, utilizing a sling mechanism to reposition the urethra for improved urinary control. Both types aim to enhance patient quality of life and reduce reliance on pads or medications.</p></p>
