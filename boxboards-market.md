<p><strong>The Boxboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Testliner</li><li>Kraftliner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boxboards are categorized mainly into testliner and kraftliner markets, each serving distinct packaging needs. Testliner is made from recycled paper and is primarily used as a top layer in corrugated boxes, providing strength and cushioning. Conversely, kraftliner, made from virgin wood pulp, is stronger and used as the outer layer of high-quality corrugated containers. Both types cater to industries such as retail and food, balancing cost, sustainability, and durability requirements for effective product transportation and presentation.</p></p>
