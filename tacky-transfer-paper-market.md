<p><p>Tacky transfer paper is used in various printing methods, each catering to specific applications. Laser printing involves high-temperature techniques that adhere toner to the paper, resulting in sharp images. Inkjet printing relies on liquid ink and offers vibrant, detailed prints, ideal for intricate designs. Lithography printing, a traditional method, utilizes plates to transfer ink onto paper, ensuring high-quality prints for larger runs. Each type of tacky transfer paper is designed to optimize the performance of these printing methods, enhancing durability and clarity.</p></p>
