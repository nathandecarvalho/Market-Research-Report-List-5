<p><p>The screen mesh market is categorized into three main types: gravity type, vacuum type, and pressurized type. Gravity type uses the natural force of gravity to facilitate material separation, making it suitable for lighter materials. Vacuum type employs suction to draw material through the mesh, effective for fine particles and liquid filtration. Pressurized type utilizes air or liquid pressure to enhance the separation process, ideal for high-density materials. Each type serves different industrial applications based on specific separation needs.</p></p>
