<p><p>Extrusion gear pumps are specialized positive displacement pumps used primarily in the extrusion process of materials such as plastics and polymers. These pumps create a consistent flow of material by utilizing two interlocking gears that efficiently transport viscous fluids through a system, ensuring uniform pressure and minimizing pulsation. Due to their accuracy and reliability, they are essential in various applications, including the manufacturing of films, sheets, and profiles.</p><p>The Extrusion Gear Pumps Market is on an upward trajectory, driven by increasing demand in sectors such as packaging, automotive, and construction.
