<p><ul><li>UV Tape</li><li>Non-UV Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor packaging cut tape market is categorized into two main types: UV tape and non-UV tape. UV tape is known for its ability to protect semiconductor devices during manufacturing processes and is cured using ultraviolet light, offering strong adhesion and clean removal. Non-UV tape, on the other hand, does not require UV light for curing and is utilized in various applications where high temperature and moisture resistance are critical. Both types play essential roles in ensuring the integrity of semiconductor components.</p></p>
