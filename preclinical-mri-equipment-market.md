<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Preclinical MRI Equipment</li><li>Superconductive Preclinical MRI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The preclinical MRI equipment market comprises two main types: permanent magnet and superconductive MRI systems. Permanent magnet preclinical MRI equipment uses fixed magnets to produce images, offering cost-effective and compact solutions suitable for small animal imaging. In contrast, superconductive preclinical MRI equipment relies on superconducting materials that generate strong magnetic fields, resulting in superior image quality and sensitivity. This type is ideal for detailed studies requiring high-resolution imaging, making it valuable for advanced research applications in life sciences.</p></p>
