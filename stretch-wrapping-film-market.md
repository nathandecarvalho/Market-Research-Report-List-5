<p><p>Stretch wrapping film is primarily categorized into three types: PE (Polyethylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and others. PE is the most popular choice due to its high elasticity, strength, and resistance to punctures, making it ideal for a wide range of applications. PVC offers excellent clarity and is often used for bundling products, although it lacks the stretchability of PE. Other materials include advanced blends and bio-based films, which cater to specific needs such as sustainability and specialized packaging requirements.</p></p>
