<p><p>Data Center Monitoring and Management Systems are categorized into two primary types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based systems leverage remote servers for data storage and processing, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance costs. They enable real-time monitoring and management from anywhere with internet access. In contrast, On-Premises systems are installed locally within the data center, providing complete control over security and data handling. These systems are favored by organizations needing tailored configurations and compliance with stringent data regulations.</p></p>
