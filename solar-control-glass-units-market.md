<p><p>The solar control glass units market comprises two main types: absorbing solar control glass and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass is designed to absorb solar radiation, reducing heat transmission while allowing natural light to pass through. In contrast, reflective solar control glass features a metallic coating that reflects a significant portion of solar energy, minimizing glare and enhancing internal comfort. Both types serve to improve energy efficiency in buildings and protect interiors from UV damage.</p></p>
