<p><p>The Negative Photosensitive Materials market comprises two primary types: Thermal Curing and UV Curing. Thermal Curing involves materials that harden upon exposure to heat, making them useful for applications requiring higher temperature resistance. Conversely, UV Curing utilizes ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, allowing for quicker processing times and enhanced precision in applications like photolithography and coatings. Both types serve specialized industries such as semiconductors, electronics, and aerospace, catering to differing performance and processing needs.</p></p>
