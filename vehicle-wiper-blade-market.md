<p><p>The vehicle wiper blade market features three main types: boneless wiper blades, bone wiper blades, and hybrid wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades, designed without traditional metal frameworks, offer a sleek, aerodynamic shape and improved performance in various weather conditions. Bone wiper blades, which incorporate a metal framework for support, are known for their durability and effective cleaning. Hybrid wiper blades combine features of both, providing the flexibility and smoothness of boneless designs with the structural sturdiness of bone blades, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
