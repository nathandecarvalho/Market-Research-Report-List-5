<p><p>The masking tape market includes various types based on material composition. Polyester-based tapes offer high tensile strength, durability, and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for industrial applications. Polyimide-based tapes excel in extreme temperatures and are commonly used in electronics and aerospace. Glass-cloth tapes provide excellent insulation and mechanical strength, suitable for high-temperature environments. Vinyl tapes are flexible, weather-resistant, and often used in automotive and construction. Other types include specialty tapes tailored for unique applications, such as low-adhesion or high-performance needs.</p></p>
