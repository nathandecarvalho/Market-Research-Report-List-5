<p><p>The automotive shielding system market comprises two primary types: EMI shielding and heat shielding. EMI shielding focuses on protecting electronic components from electromagnetic interference, ensuring reliable vehicle performance and compliance with regulatory standards. Heat shielding, on the other hand, isolates sensitive parts from excessive temperatures generated by the engine and exhaust systems, enhancing safety and efficiency. Both shielding types are essential in modern vehicles to improve operation, longevity, and overall safety of automotive systems against external and internal threats.</p></p>
