<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cocoa Solids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Cocoa Solid</li><li>Dutch-processed Cocoa Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cocoa solids are categorized mainly into natural cocoa solids and Dutch-processed cocoa solids. Natural cocoa solids are derived from unalkalized cocoa beans, retaining a strong chocolate flavor and acidic profile, commonly used in baking and confectionery. In contrast, Dutch-processed cocoa solids undergo an alkalization process, resulting in a milder flavor and darker color, often favored in recipes requiring less acidity. Each type serves distinct purposes in the culinary industry, impacting flavor, color, and overall product quality.</p></p>
