<p><p>Battery grade solvents are essential for the lithium-ion battery manufacturing process, optimizing electrolyte formulations. DMC (Dimethyl Carbonate) and EMC (Ethyl Methyl Carbonate) are favored for their high solvency and electrochemical stability, enhancing battery performance. EC (Ethylene Carbonate) and DEC (Diethyl Carbonate) provide excellent viscosity and ion conductivity. PC (Propylene Carbonate) offers a balance of stability and conductivity, making it suitable for high-performance batteries. Each solvent plays a crucial role in improving energy density, safety, and overall battery efficiency.</p></p>
