<p><p>The synthetic rubber coated fabrics market is segmented into various types, each offering unique properties and applications. Silicone rubber offers high thermal stability and flexibility, commonly used in automotive and medical sectors. Butyl rubber is known for its airtight properties, suitable for inflatable products. Nitrile rubber is resistant to oils and chemicals, often used in industrial gloves. EPDM rubber offers weather resistance, commonly used in roofing and automotive seals, while "Others" encompass various less common materials.</p></p>
