<p><p>The animal feed prebiotics market is primarily categorized into four types: Inulin, Fructooligosaccharides (FOS), Isomaltooligosaccharides (IMO), and others. Inulin, a soluble fiber, promotes gut health by enhancing beneficial bacteria. FOS is a low-calorie sugar that aids digestion and boosts nutrient absorption. IMO, derived from starch, supports immunity and promotes gut microbiota diversity. The "Others" category encompasses additional prebiotics like galactooligosaccharides and xylooligosaccharides, contributing to overall animal health and growth while improving feed efficiency.</p></p>
