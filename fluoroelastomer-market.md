<p><p>Fluoroelastomers are synthetic rubber materials known for their excellent resistance to heat, chemicals, and oil. The market is divided into three main types: Fluorocarbon Elastomers, which offer high temperature and chemical resistance; Fluorosilicone Elastomers, which have improved flexibility and fuel resistance compared to traditional fluoroelastomers; and Perfluoroelastomers, which provide superior performance in extreme environments with high temperatures, aggressive chemicals, and harsh operating conditions. Each type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
