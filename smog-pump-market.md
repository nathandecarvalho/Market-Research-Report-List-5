<p><p>A smog pump, also known as an air injection pump, is a component used in automotive emissions control systems. It injects fresh air into the exhaust stream to help reduce harmful emissions by promoting the oxidation of unburned hydrocarbons and carbon monoxide into less harmful substances like carbon dioxide and water. Smog pumps are primarily used in older vehicles equipped with internal combustion engines to comply with emission regulations.</p><p>The Smog Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing environmental regulations aimed at reducing vehicle emissions, particularly in developing regions. The demand for smog pumps is also supported by the need for retrofitting older vehicles to meet stricter emission standards. Additionally, advancements in pump technology, such as improved durability and efficiency, are contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Key trends in the smog pump market include the integration of smart technologies for better emission control and the development of lightweight, compact pumps to enhance vehicle performance. The aftermarket segment is witnessing significant growth due to the rising demand for replacement parts. Overall, the market remains robust, driven by regulatory pressures and technological innovations.</p></p>
