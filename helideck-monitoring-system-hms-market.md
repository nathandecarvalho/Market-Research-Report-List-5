<p><p>The Helideck Monitoring System (HMS) ensures safe helicopter operations on offshore platforms by monitoring environmental conditions. It encompasses various market types: Motion sensors detect deck movement; wind sensors measure wind speed and direction; meteorology sensors provide atmospheric data like temperature and visibility; GPS systems track helicopter positioning; and gyro sensors monitor the orientation of the helideck. Together, these components enhance safety, provide real-time data, and support decision-making for helicopter landings and departures in challenging offshore environments.</p></p>
