<p><p>The Rigid Vinyl (PVC) Film market is segmented into two main types: Clear Rigid Vinyl and Opaque Rigid Vinyl. Clear Rigid Vinyl is characterized by its transparency and is commonly used for packaging, window clings, and display applications, allowing for visibility of contents. Opaque Rigid Vinyl, on the other hand, lacks transparency and is often utilized in applications requiring privacy, aesthetic appeal, or branding, such as signage, protective covers, and containers. Both types serve distinct market needs across various industries.</p></p>
