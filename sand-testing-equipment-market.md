<p><p>The sand testing equipment market comprises various types, including sand samplers, sand washers, and permeability meters. Sand samplers are used to collect representative samples from different locations for analysis. Sand washers remove impurities and ensure the quality of sand before use. Permeability meters assess the flow rate of liquids through sand samples, crucial for understanding soil behavior. Other equipment may include moisture meters and compaction tests, all contributing to the evaluation and quality control of sand for construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
