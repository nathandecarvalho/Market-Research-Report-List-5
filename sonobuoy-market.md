<p><ul><li>Active Sonobuoy</li><li>Passive Sonobuoy</li><li>Special Purpose Sonobuoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sonobuoys are classified into three main types based on their functionality: Active, Passive, and Special Purpose. Active Sonobuoys transmit acoustic signals and actively search for underwater targets. Passive Sonobuoys listen for acoustic signals without transmitting any of their own. Special Purpose Sonobuoys are designed for specific applications, such as detecting submarines or monitoring marine environments. Each type serves a unique role in underwater surveillance and monitoring, catering to different needs and requirements in the defense and research sectors.</p></p>
