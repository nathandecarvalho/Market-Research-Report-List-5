<p><p>The ductile iron castings market is primarily divided into vertical and horizontal molding types, each serving distinct production needs. Vertical molding involves molds positioned upright, allowing for efficient sand filling and rapid cooling, ideal for complex shapes and large volumes. Conversely, horizontal molding features molds laid flat, facilitating larger castings and providing easier access for finishing processes. Both molding techniques enhance productivity and casting quality, catering to various industries such as automotive, construction, and machinery with specific manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
