Steam turbines utilize high-pressure steam produced from biomass combustion to generate electricity. Boilers are responsible for burning biomass to produce steam. Generators convert mechanical energy from turbines into electrical energy. Pumps are crucial for transporting fluid, often used in the biomass process to move water or biofuels.
