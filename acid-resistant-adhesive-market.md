<p><p>The acid-resistant adhesive market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that require no additional components, offering convenience and ease of use. They typically cure when exposed to air or heat. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed before application, providing superior bonding strength and resistance to harsh chemicals. These distinct types cater to various industrial applications requiring specific performance characteristics in acid-prone environments.</p></p>
