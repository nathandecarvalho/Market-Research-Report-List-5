<p><p>Ceramic coatings in the thermal spray market are classified into three main types: nitrides, carbides, and oxides. Nitrides, such as titanium nitride, offer high hardness and wear resistance, making them ideal for cutting tools. Carbides, such as tungsten carbide, are renowned for their exceptional durability and thermal stability, suitable for demanding industrial applications. Oxides, like alumina and zirconia, are valued for their thermal insulation and corrosion resistance, commonly used in aerospace and automotive sectors for protective coatings.</p></p>
