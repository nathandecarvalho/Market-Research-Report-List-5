<p><p>The supported metal catalyst market consists of two primary types: single metal catalysts and multimetallic catalysts. Single metal catalysts utilize one type of metal, such as platinum or palladium, to facilitate chemical reactions, offering efficient catalytic activity for specific processes. Multimetallic catalysts, on the other hand, combine two or more metals, enabling enhanced catalytic properties and broadened reaction pathways. This multifaceted approach often leads to improved performance and selectivity, making multimetallic catalysts increasingly valuable in various industrial applications.</p></p>
