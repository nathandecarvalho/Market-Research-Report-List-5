<p><p>The lumber mill market can be categorized into two main types: softwood lumber and hardwood lumber. Hardwood lumber, sourced from deciduous trees such as oak and maple, is denser, more durable, and often used in furniture and cabinetry. Each type serves distinct applications and preferences in both residential and commercial markets.</p></p>
