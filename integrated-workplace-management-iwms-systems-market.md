<p><p>Integrated Workplace Management Systems (IWMS) can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based IWMS allows organizations to access tools and data via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. This model benefits organizations with remote access and automated updates. In contrast, on-premises IWMS involves installing software directly on local servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance efforts. Each approach caters to different operational needs and resources.</p></p>
