<p><p>Wafer back grinding tapes are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, specifically for thinning wafers after dicing. The market is segmented into UV type and non-UV type. UV type tapes utilize ultraviolet light for curing the adhesive, providing strong adhesion and easy removal without residue. Non-UV type tapes rely on thermal or pressure-sensitive adhesion, offering versatility in application but sometimes challenging removal. Both types cater to different manufacturing processes, with UV types preferred for precision and cleanliness in advanced applications.</p></p>
