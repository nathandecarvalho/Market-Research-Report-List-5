<p><p>The transmission coolers market comprises two main types: fan cooling and water cooling. Fan cooling type utilizes electric or mechanical fans to enhance airflow over the cooler, efficiently dissipating heat from the transmission fluid. In contrast, water cooling type employs engine coolant to absorb heat from the transmission fluid, providing effective temperature regulation. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal transmission performance, prolonging vehicle life, and enhancing efficiency in various automotive applications, including passenger cars and heavy-duty vehicles.</p></p>
