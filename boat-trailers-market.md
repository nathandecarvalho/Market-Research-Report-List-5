<p><ul><li>Bunk</li><li>Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat trailer market primarily segments into two types: bunk and roller trailers. Bunk trailers utilize a set of padded bunks to support the boat's hull, providing stability during transport and launching. They are ideal for larger, heavier boats. Conversely, roller trailers employ a series of rollers, allowing easier loading and unloading, making them suitable for smaller or lighter boats. Each type caters to specific boating needs and preferences, impacting user experience and convenience on the water.</p></p>
