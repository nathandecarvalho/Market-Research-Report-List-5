<p><p>The bio fertilizer market is categorized into three main types: bacterial, actinomycetes, and fungi fertilizers. Bacterial fertilizers utilize beneficial bacteria to enhance soil health and promote nutrient availability. Actinomycetes fertilizers harness specific filamentous bacteria that contribute to soil fertility and organic matter decomposition. Fungi fertilizers consist of mycorrhizal and other beneficial fungi, improving plant root systems and nutrient absorption. Together, these bio fertilizers offer environmentally friendly alternatives to synthetic fertilizers, fostering sustainable agricultural practices and enhancing crop yields.</p></p>
