<p><ul><li>Powder-Free Disposable Nitrile Gloves</li><li>Powdered Disposable Nitrile Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable nitrile gloves market is primarily divided into two types: powder-free and powdered gloves. Powder-free disposable nitrile gloves are designed for users who prefer a smooth, non-irritating fit, making them ideal for sensitive skin and healthcare applications. They are typically more expensive but offer enhanced comfort. Powdered disposable nitrile gloves, on the other hand, are coated with a powder to aid in donning and doffing. While generally less expensive, they can cause skin irritation and are less favored in medical settings.</p></p>
