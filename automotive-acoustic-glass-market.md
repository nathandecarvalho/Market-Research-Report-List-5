<p><p>The Automotive Acoustic Glass market primarily consists of two key types: Double Deck Glass and Three Layers of Glass. Double Deck Glass features two layers of glass separated by a sound-damping interlayer, effectively reducing noise while maintaining structural integrity. In contrast, Three Layers of Glass adds an additional layer, further enhancing sound insulation and vibration dampening. Both options significantly improve passenger comfort by minimizing external noise, making them increasingly popular in premium and luxury vehicles, where acoustic performance is a priority.</p></p>
