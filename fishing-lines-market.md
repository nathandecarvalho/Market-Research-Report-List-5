<p><p>The fishing lines market primarily consists of two main types: braided and monofilament lines. Braided lines are known for their strength and sensitivity, made from multiple strands of fiber woven together, providing high casting distance and durability. In contrast, monofilament lines are single-stranded and offer versatility, ease of handling, and a degree of stretch, making them ideal for various fishing techniques. Each type caters to different angling needs, influencing choices based on fishing conditions and target species.</p></p>
