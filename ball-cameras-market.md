<p><strong>The Ball Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Cameras</li><li>IP Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball cameras market can be categorized into two main types: analog cameras and IP (Internet Protocol) cameras. Analog cameras transmit video signals over coaxial cables, typically offering lower resolution and requiring direct connections to recording devices. On the other hand, IP cameras utilize network connections to stream high-quality video, allowing for remote access and advanced features like motion detection and analytics. This differentiation shapes their application profiles, with analog being more traditional and cost-effective, while IP cameras provide enhanced versatility and scalability.</p></p>
