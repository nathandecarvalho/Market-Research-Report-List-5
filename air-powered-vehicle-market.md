<p><p>Air-powered vehicles utilize compressed air as their primary energy source, offering an eco-friendly alternative to traditional fuel-based transportation. The market is segmented into **Single Energy Mode** and **Dual Energy Mode**. Single Energy Mode vehicles rely solely on compressed air for propulsion, making them lightweight and simple but with limited range. Dual Energy Mode vehicles combine compressed air with an auxiliary energy source (e.g., electric or fuel), enhancing range and efficiency, making them more versatile for diverse applications. Both types aim to reduce emissions and promote sustainable mobility.</p></p>
