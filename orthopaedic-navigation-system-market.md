<p><ul><li>Optical Navigation Systems</li><li>Electromagnetic Navigation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopaedic navigation system market comprises primarily two types: optical navigation systems and electromagnetic navigation systems. Optical navigation systems utilize cameras and infrared technology to track instruments and bone landmarks, offering high precision with minimal interference. In contrast, electromagnetic navigation systems leverage electromagnetic fields to determine the position of surgical tools, allowing for flexibility in varying environments. Both systems enhance surgical accuracy and outcomes, catering to the evolving demands of orthopaedic procedures and patient care.</p></p>
