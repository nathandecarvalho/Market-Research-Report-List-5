<p><p>The high-speed surgical drill market is divided into two main types: pneumatic and electric high-speed surgical drills. Pneumatic high-speed surgical drills operate using compressed air, providing a lightweight and high-speed option favored in various surgical procedures. They offer precision and ease of use but may require an air source. In contrast, electric high-speed surgical drills are powered by electricity, offering consistent torque and speed control, making them suitable for intricate surgeries where precision is critical. Both types cater to specific surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
