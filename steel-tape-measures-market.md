<p><p>The steel tape measures market includes various types, primarily spring return pocket tape measures and long tape measures. Spring return pocket tape measures are compact, portable tools designed for quick measurements, often used in DIY and construction tasks. They feature a retractable tape that springs back automatically when released. Long tape measures, on the other hand, are typically used for measuring larger distances in fields like surveying and construction, offering extended lengths and greater durability for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
