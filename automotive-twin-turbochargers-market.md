<p><ul><li>Tandem Turbo</li><li>Parallel Turbo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive twin turbocharger market includes two main types: Tandem Turbo and Parallel Turbo systems. Tandem Turbo setups use two turbos of different sizes, with a smaller turbo providing quick low-end boost and a larger turbo handling high-end power, ensuring smooth performance across RPM ranges. Parallel Turbo systems employ two identical turbos, each feeding half the engine cylinders, delivering balanced power and efficiency. Both systems enhance engine performance, reduce turbo lag, and improve fuel efficiency, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
