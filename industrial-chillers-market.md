<p><p>The industrial chillers market comprises three main types: air chillers, water chillers, and evaporative condensed chillers. Air chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for facilities with adequate ventilation. Water chillers utilize water to absorb and transfer heat, providing efficient cooling, especially in large-scale applications. Evaporative condensed chillers combine the principles of refrigeration and evaporation, offering high efficiency by using less water and energy, ideal for industries seeking sustainable cooling solutions.</p></p>
