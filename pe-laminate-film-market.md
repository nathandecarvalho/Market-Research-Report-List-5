<p><p>The PE laminate film market is segmented into three main types: duplex, triplex, and other laminates. Duplex films consist of two layers, offering a balance between durability and flexibility, making them suitable for packaging applications. Triplex films feature three layers, providing enhanced barrier properties, which are ideal for products requiring superior protection. The "other" category encompasses various specialized laminates that meet unique requirements, such as specific thicknesses or additional functionalities, catering to diverse industries and applications.</p></p>
