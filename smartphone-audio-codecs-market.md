<p><p>The smartphone audio codecs market is divided into two main types: lossless and lossy compression. Lossless compression retains the original audio quality during data compression, allowing for high-fidelity sound reproduction without any loss of detail, appealing to audiophiles. In contrast, lossy compression sacrifices some audio quality for reduced file size, making it more suitable for everyday users who prioritize storage efficiency and streaming speed. Both types cater to different consumer needs, shaping their respective market segments.</p></p>
