<p><p>The temporary cardiac pacing wires market features three main types: unipolar pacing wires, bipolar pacing wires, and quadripolar pacing wires. Unipolar pacing wires consist of a single electrode and are typically simpler and less expensive. Bipolar pacing wires have two electrodes, allowing for more precise stimulation and reduced interference. Quadripolar pacing wires feature four electrodes, providing enhanced pacing flexibility and options for dual-site stimulation. Each type caters to specific patient needs and procedural requirements in cardiac care.</p></p>
