<p><ul><li>Single Fiber Fusion Splicer</li><li>Ribbon Fiber Fusion Splicer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The splicing machine market is primarily categorized into single fiber fusion splicers and ribbon fiber fusion splicers. Single fiber fusion splicers are designed for connecting individual optical fibers, providing precision and efficiency for general installations. In contrast, ribbon fiber fusion splicers can splice multiple fibers simultaneously, making them ideal for high-capacity applications, such as telecommunications and data centers. Both types enhance the performance and reliability of fiber optic networks, catering to diverse industry needs and increasing demand for optical connectivity.</p></p>
