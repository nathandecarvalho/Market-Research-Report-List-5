<p><p>The lithium-ion battery market features various types tailored for specific applications. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LCO) offers high energy density, ideal for portable electronics. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) provides excellent thermal stability and safety, making it suitable for electric vehicles. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) combines high energy density with longevity, used in electric vehicles and storage systems. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) offers enhanced thermal stability, primarily for power tools. Lithium Titanate (LTO) supports rapid charging, whereas Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt (NMC) balances performance, cost, and safety.</p></p>
