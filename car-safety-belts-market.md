<p><p>Car safety belts are essential for passenger protection. **Two-point safety belts** secure the waist, commonly used in rear seats. **Three-point safety belts** combine lap and shoulder straps, offering enhanced safety and widely used in front seats. **Four-point safety belts** add an additional shoulder strap, providing extra stability, often in performance or racing vehicles. **Others** include advanced systems like five-point or six-point harnesses, designed for maximum restraint in specialized applications. Each type caters to specific safety needs, balancing comfort and protection.</p></p>
