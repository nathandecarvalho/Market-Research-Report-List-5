<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Testing Machines market is divided into two primary types: Hard X-Ray Machines and Soft X-Ray Machines. Hard X-Ray Machines operate at higher energy levels, making them suitable for detecting internal flaws in dense materials like metals, offering high-resolution imaging. In contrast, Soft X-Ray Machines, operating at lower energy levels, are ideal for examining softer materials, such as plastics and textiles, allowing for detailed surface inspections without damaging the sample. Both types enhance quality control and safety in various industries.</p></p>
