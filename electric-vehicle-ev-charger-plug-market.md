<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) Charger Plug market is primarily divided into two types: DC (direct current) and AC (alternating current) chargers. DC chargers deliver high-speed charging directly to the vehicle's battery, making them ideal for fast-charging stations, often found along highways. AC chargers, on the other hand, supply power that needs to be converted into DC by the vehicle, making them suitable for home and workplace charging where longer charging times are acceptable. Each type serves different user needs and charging scenarios.</p></p>
