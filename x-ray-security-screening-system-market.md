<p><p>The X-Ray Security Screening System market comprises three primary types: X-Ray Generators, X-Ray Detectors, and Image Processing Systems. X-Ray Generators produce the radiation required for scanning objects, ensuring optimal penetration and clarity. X-Ray Detectors capture the transmitted X-rays, converting them into visible images for analysis. Image Processing Systems enhance these images, applying algorithms to identify anomalies and improve clarity. Together, these components facilitate efficient security screening in airports, borders, and public venues, ensuring safety and threat detection.</p></p>
