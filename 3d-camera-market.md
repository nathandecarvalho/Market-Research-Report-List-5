<p><ul><li>Target Free Camera</li><li>Target Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D camera market is primarily divided into two types: Target-Free Cameras and Target Cameras. Target-Free Cameras operate without the need for a specific reference point, utilizing advanced algorithms to capture depth and spatial data in real-time, ideal for applications such as virtual reality and gaming. In contrast, Target Cameras rely on identifiable markers or objects within the scene to accurately measure depth, commonly used in industries like robotics and augmented reality for precise object tracking and interaction.</p></p>
