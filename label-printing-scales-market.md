<p><p>Label printing scales come in two primary types: single monitor and double monitor systems. Single monitor scales feature one display that shows both the weight and label information, making them straightforward and user-friendly for basic operations. In contrast, double monitor scales are equipped with two screens — one for the operator and another for customers. This setup enhances transparency and communication, allowing customers to see the weight and pricing details, which can improve the overall shopping experience and increase efficiency at checkout.</p></p>
