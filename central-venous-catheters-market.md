Double-lumen catheters allow simultaneous infusion and blood sampling. Triple-lumen catheters provide multiple channels for diverse applications, such as administering fluids, medications, and blood products concurrently. Other types may include specialized catheters designed for unique clinical needs, enhancing versatility and functionality in patient treatment and monitoring.</p></p>
