<p><ul><li>Infrared Remote</li><li>Bluetooth Remote</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal TV Remote Market comprises two primary types: Infrared (IR) remotes and Bluetooth remotes. Infrared remotes use line-of-sight technology, requiring a direct path to the device they control, making them widely compatible but limited in range. Conversely, Bluetooth remotes offer greater flexibility, allowing users to control devices from a distance without needing direct visibility. This adaptability enhances user experience, especially in smart homes, where multiple devices are operated seamlessly, and contributes to the growing demand for advanced universal remotes.</p></p>
