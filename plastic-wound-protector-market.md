<p><p>The plastic wound protector market is categorized into two main types: ring-based and prong-based wound protectors. Ring-based wound protectors feature a circular design that provides a secure barrier around the surgical site, minimizing contamination and facilitating easier access for healthcare professionals. In contrast, prong-based wound protectors utilize multiple prongs to anchor the device in place, offering flexibility in positioning and enhanced stability. Both types are essential in surgical settings to enhance patient safety and improve procedural outcomes.</p></p>
