<p><p>Dental X-ray system market includes two main types of imaging: intraoral X-ray imaging and extraoral X-ray imaging. Intraoral X-ray imaging involves placing a sensor inside the mouth to capture detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures. On the other hand, extraoral X-ray imaging captures broader images of the entire mouth, jaw, and skull from outside the mouth. Both types of imaging play a crucial role in diagnosing dental issues and planning treatments, contributing to the overall growth of the dental X-ray system market.</p></p>
