SCBIs are designed for convenience, featuring a built-in culture medium that provides immediate results after exposure to sterilization processes. In contrast, Biological Indicator Strips consist of a spore strip that requires external incubation for result interpretation. Both types are essential for validating sterilization efficacy, ensuring patient safety, and maintaining compliance in healthcare and laboratory environments.</p></p>
