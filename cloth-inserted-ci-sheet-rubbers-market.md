In contrast, Multilayer Cloth incorporates multiple layers of fabric, enhancing resilience, flexibility, and resistance to wear and tear. This multilayer construction makes it suitable for more demanding applications, providing improved performance in harsh environments. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, manufacturing, and packaging.</p></p>
