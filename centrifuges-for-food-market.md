<p><p>Centrifuges for food come in two main market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical centrifuges are designed to separate liquids from solids by spinning them at high speeds along a vertical axis. They are commonly used in industries like dairy and pharmaceuticals. Horizontal centrifuges, on the other hand, use a horizontal axis rotation to separate liquids of different densities.
