<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fume hoods market is primarily categorized into ducted and ductless types. Ducted fume hoods ventilate hazardous fumes outdoors through ductwork, offering effective containment and protection for lab environments. They are ideal for applications involving highly toxic chemicals. In contrast, ductless fume hoods use built-in filtration systems to purify air and recirculate it within the laboratory, making them suitable for environments where duct installation is impractical. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in chemical handling and laboratory operations.</p></p>
