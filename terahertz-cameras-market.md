<p><p>The terahertz cameras market comprises two primary types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects natural terahertz radiation emitted from objects, allowing for non-invasive inspection, particularly useful in security and quality control applications. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves generating terahertz signals that interact with the target, enhancing imaging capabilities for detailed analysis. Together, these technologies offer versatile solutions across various sectors, including medical diagnostics, telecommunications, and material characterization.</p></p>
