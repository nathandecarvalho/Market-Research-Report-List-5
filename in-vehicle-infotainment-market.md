<p><p>The In-Vehicle Infotainment market includes three main types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated. Embedded infotainment systems are built into the vehicle and offer seamless integration with the car's features. Tethered systems rely on external devices like smartphones for connectivity and functionality. Integrated systems combine infotainment with other vehicle controls, providing a centralized interface for various functions. Each type caters to different preferences and needs, offering a range of options for consumers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
