<p><p>The Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) market comprises various types, each offering distinct insulation properties and applications. EPS panels, made from expanded polystyrene, provide lightweight and cost-effective insulation. PUR/PIR panels, crafted from polyurethane or polyisocyanurate, offer superior thermal performance and fire resistance. Glass wool panels, known for their sound insulation and eco-friendliness, are suitable for residential and commercial constructions. Other types may include foam core or metal-faced panels, catering to specialized building requirements, enhancing energy efficiency and structural integrity.</p></p>
