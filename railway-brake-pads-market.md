<p><p>The railway brake pads market comprises primarily two types: composite brake pads and powder metallurgy brake pads. Composite brake pads are made from a mixture of materials, such as fibers, resins, and additives, offering excellent wear resistance and noise reduction. In contrast, powder metallurgy brake pads are crafted from metal powders, which are sintered to achieve high strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and efficiency in railway operations.</p></p>
