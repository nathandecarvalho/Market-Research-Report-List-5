<p><p>Electronic Medical Records (EMR) software solutions are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based EMR systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing healthcare providers to access patient records securely from any location via the internet. This model is often more cost-effective and scalable. In contrast, on-premise EMR solutions are installed locally on a healthcare organization’s servers, offering greater control and customization, but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
