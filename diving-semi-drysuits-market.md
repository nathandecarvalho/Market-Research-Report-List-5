<p><p>The diving semi-drysuits market is primarily divided into two types: hot water semi-drysuits and cold water semi-drysuits. Hot water semi-drysuits are designed for warmer environments and feature lighter materials, providing flexibility and comfort while offering thermal protection. In contrast, cold water semi-drysuits are constructed with thicker insulation and watertight seals to safeguard against chilling temperatures, ensuring enhanced thermal retention for divers in colder aquatic conditions. Both types cater to specific diving needs and environmental challenges.</p></p>
