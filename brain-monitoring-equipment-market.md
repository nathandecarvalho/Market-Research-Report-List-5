<p><p>The brain monitoring equipment market encompasses various devices essential for assessing brain function and health. Electroencephalography (EEG) devices record electrical activity, while Magnetoencephalography (MEG) measures magnetic fields generated by neuronal activity. Transcranial Doppler (TCD) devices evaluate cerebral blood flow. Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitors track pressure within the skull, and cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels in the brain. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) and Computerized Tomography (CT) devices provide detailed images of brain structures, while sleep monitoring devices assess sleep patterns and disorders.</p></p>
