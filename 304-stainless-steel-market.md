<p><p>The 304 stainless steel market is categorized primarily into two types: 304 and 304L. The 304 grade is known for its excellent corrosion resistance and high tensile strength, making it suitable for a wide range of applications, from food processing to chemical storage. On the other hand, 304L offers lower carbon content, reducing the risk of carbide precipitation during welding, thus enhancing its suitability for welded structures. Both variants are widely used in industries requiring durability and reliability.</p></p>
