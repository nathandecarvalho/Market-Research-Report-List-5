<p><p>The medical external defibrillator market consists primarily of two types: semi-automatic and automatic defibrillators. Semi-automatic defibrillators require user intervention to deliver an electric shock, guiding operators through voice prompts for the process. In contrast, automatic defibrillators analyze the heart rhythm and deliver shocks automatically, requiring minimal user involvement. Both types are crucial for emergency care, enhancing survival rates during cardiac arrest incidents by providing timely and effective defibrillation, with growing demand in hospitals, public spaces, and emergency services.</p></p>
