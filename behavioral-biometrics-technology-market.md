<p><p>Behavioral biometrics technology encompasses various methods for user authentication based on unique behavioral patterns. Keystroke dynamics analyzes typing speed and rhythm. Gait analysis assesses an individual’s walking style to identify or verify identity. Signature analysis examines the unique characteristics of a person’s signature, including pressure and stroke order. Voice recognition captures vocal traits, such as pitch and tone, for authentication. Together, these types provide robust security by leveraging individual behavioral patterns, making systems harder to spoof compared to traditional biometrics.</p></p>
