<p><p>The cardiac monitoring products market encompasses various types, including Resting ECG, Stress ECG, Holter ECG, and others. Resting ECG captures heart activity while the patient is at rest, providing baseline data. Stress ECG monitors heart function during physical activity, identifying potential issues under stress. Holter ECG offers continuous monitoring over 24 hours or more, allowing for the detection of irregularities during daily activities. Other products may include event recorders and implantable monitors, catering to diverse cardiac diagnostic needs.</p></p>
