<p><p>The active-matrix liquid-crystal display (AMLCD) market primarily features two types: polycrystalline silicon (p-Si) and amorphous silicon (a-Si). Polycrystalline silicon displays offer higher performance, faster refresh rates, and improved power efficiency, making them suitable for high-resolution applications like televisions and monitors. In contrast, amorphous silicon displays are more cost-effective and commonly used in lower-resolution applications such as mobile devices and basic screens. Both types cater to diverse consumer demands, influencing overall market growth and innovation.</p></p>
