<p><p>Color masterbatch is a concentrated mixture of pigments or additives encapsulated into a carrier resin, used to impart color and enhance properties in plastic products during manufacturing. It is widely utilized across industries such as packaging, automotive, construction, and consumer goods due to its cost-effectiveness, consistency, and ease of use. The market for color masterbatch is driven by the growing demand for colored plastics in various applications, including flexible packaging, injection molding, and extrusion.</p><p>The Color Masterbatch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period.
