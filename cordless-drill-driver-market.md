<p><p>The cordless drill driver market comprises primarily two types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic drill drivers utilize compressed air as their power source, offering high torque and speed, ideal for heavy-duty applications. However, their reliance on an air compressor limits portability. In contrast, electric drill drivers, powered by rechargeable batteries, provide greater convenience and mobility, making them suitable for both professional and DIY tasks. The electric market is predominant due to its versatility and ease of use in various settings.</p></p>
