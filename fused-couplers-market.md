<p><strong>The Fused Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multi-mode Couplers</li><li>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fused couplers market is categorized into three main types: Single Mode Couplers, Multi-mode Couplers, and Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers. Single mode couplers are designed for use in single-mode fibers, offering low loss and high performance for long-distance communication. Multi-mode couplers work with multi-mode fibers, accommodating multiple light paths for shorter distances. PM couplers are engineered to maintain the polarization of light, crucial for applications requiring specific polarization states. Each type plays a vital role in optical network performance.</p></p>
