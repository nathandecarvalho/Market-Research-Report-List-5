<p><ul><li>Lift Gas Spring (Non-locking)</li><li>Lockable Gas Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car gas springs market primarily features two types: lift gas springs (non-locking) and lockable gas springs. Lift gas springs are designed to assist in lifting and supporting vehicle components, providing smooth motion without a locking mechanism, allowing them to return to a closed position automatically. In contrast, lockable gas springs offer adjustable tension and can secure positions, making them ideal for applications requiring stability and safety, such as in hoods or tailgates. Both types enhance vehicle functionality and user experience.</p></p>
