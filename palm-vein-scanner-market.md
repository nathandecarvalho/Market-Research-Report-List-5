<p><p>The palm vein scanner market is primarily categorized into two types: palm vein biometrics and finger vein biometrics. Palm vein biometrics utilize unique vein patterns in the palm for identification, offering high accuracy and security. This type is commonly used in access control and financial transactions. Finger vein biometrics, on the other hand, analyze the vein patterns in a user's finger, providing a compact solution suitable for various applications like mobile devices and authentication systems. Both technologies enhance security through biometric verification.</p></p>
