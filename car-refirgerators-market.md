<p><ul><li>Semiconductor Type</li><li>Compressor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car refrigerator market mainly comprises two types: semiconductor and compressor types. Semiconductor refrigerators utilize thermoelectric modules for cooling, offering compact designs and lower energy consumption, making them ideal for small vehicles or short trips. In contrast, compressor refrigerators operate similarly to home fridges, using a compressor-driven refrigeration cycle to achieve lower temperatures and larger storage capacities, making them suitable for long journeys or larger vehicles. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences in mobile refrigeration.</p></p>
