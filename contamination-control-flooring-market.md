<p><p>Contamination control flooring encompasses various types designed to minimize pollutants in cleanroom environments. Rubber-cold welded flooring offers seamless protection against microbial growth. Seamless vinyl provides a non-porous surface easily cleaned. Epoxy coatings and troweled-on options create durable, resistant layers. MMA (Methyl Methacrylate) flooring cures quickly and withstands harsh chemicals. Urethane flooring offers flexibility and resistance to abrasion. Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) provides a cost-effective, easy-to-maintain alternative. Raised floors facilitate airflow and access to utilities in controlled spaces.</p></p>
