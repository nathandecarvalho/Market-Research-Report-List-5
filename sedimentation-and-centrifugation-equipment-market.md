<p><p>Sedimentation and centrifugation equipment market includes various types such as clarifiers, disk centrifuges, decanters, dissolved air flotation systems, hydrocyclones, and other equipment. Clarifiers are used for solid-liquid separation, disk centrifuges separate solids from liquids based on density, decanters are used for continuous sedimentation of solids, dissolved air flotation systems remove suspended solids from water, hydrocyclones separate particles based on size and density. Other equipment in this market includes gravity thickeners, belt presses, and drum thickeners for various sedimentation and centrifugation processes.</p></p>
