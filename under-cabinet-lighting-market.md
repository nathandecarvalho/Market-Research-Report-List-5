<p><p>The under cabinet lighting market features three primary types: incandescent, fluorescent, and LED. Fluorescent lighting provides brighter light and greater energy efficiency compared to incandescent, though color rendering can be less appealing. LED lights dominate the market due to their superior energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and versatility in color options.
