<p><p>The graphite sheet market comprises three main types: natural graphite sheets, synthetic graphite sheets, and nanocomposite graphite sheets. Natural graphite sheets are derived from naturally occurring graphite and are valued for their thermal and electrical conductivity. Synthetic graphite sheets are manufactured through high-temperature processes and offer superior consistency and purity. Nanocomposite graphite sheets combine graphite with other materials at the nanoscale, enhancing performance attributes such as strength and flexibility. Each type serves diverse applications across industries, including electronics and energy solutions.</p></p>
