<p><p>The Malfunction Indicator Light (MIL) market is categorized into two main types: Intermittent Malfunction Indicator Light and Continuous Malfunction Indicator Light. Intermittent MILs activate sporadically, signaling temporary issues that may self-resolve or recur, often complicating diagnostics. In contrast, Continuous MILs remain illuminated, indicating persistent problems that require immediate attention. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle diagnostics, influencing consumer decisions and repair services in the automotive industry, ultimately impacting vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
