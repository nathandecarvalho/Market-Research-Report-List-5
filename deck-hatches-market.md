<p><ul><li>Watertight</li><li>Non-Watertight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deck hatches market can be categorized into two main types: watertight and non-watertight hatches. Watertight hatches are designed to prevent water ingress, making them ideal for marine vessels and structures exposed to harsh weather conditions. They ensure safety and integrity during heavy seas. Non-watertight hatches, on the other hand, provide access without the same level of protection against water, suitable for applications where exposure to moisture is less critical. Both types cater to specific operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
