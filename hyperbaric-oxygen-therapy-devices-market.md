<p><p>The hyperbaric oxygen therapy devices market is primarily categorized into two types: monoplace and multiplace hyperbaric oxygen therapy devices. Monoplace devices are designed for single patients, providing a controlled environment where individuals receive concentrated oxygen in a single chamber. In contrast, multiplace devices accommodate multiple patients simultaneously, allowing for a more collaborative treatment environment, often with medical personnel present. Both types serve critical roles in treating conditions such as decompression sickness, chronic wounds, and other medical conditions benefiting from enhanced oxygen delivery.</p></p>
