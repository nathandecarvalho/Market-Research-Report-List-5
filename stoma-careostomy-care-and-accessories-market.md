<p><p>Stoma care and ostomy care involve managing an opening created during surgical procedures to divert waste from the body. The market is categorized into three main types: ileostomy, colostomy, and urostomy. Ileostomy involves diverting intestinal waste to a pouch after the ileum, suitable for inflammatory bowel disease patients. Colostomy uses the colon for waste diversion, often due to colorectal cancer. Urostomy diverts urine from the kidneys, typically for bladder issues. Accessories enhance comfort, hygiene, and lifestyle for ostomy patients.</p></p>
