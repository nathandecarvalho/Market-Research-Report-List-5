<p><p>The fire damper market comprises various types, including mechanical dampers, intumescent dampers, and air transfer fire dampers. Mechanical dampers utilize physical mechanisms to close off ducts during a fire, preventing smoke and heat spread. Intumescent dampers expand when exposed to heat, sealing openings effectively. Air transfer fire dampers facilitate the movement of air between spaces while blocking fire and smoke during emergencies. These diverse solutions enhance fire safety measures in buildings, catering to different airflow and fire protection needs.</p></p>
