<p><ul><li>Light-cure</li><li>Dual-cure</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental resin cements are essential in restorative dentistry, classified mainly into light-cure, dual-cure, and others. Light-cure cements require a curing light for polymerization, providing excellent aesthetics and bond strength but limiting depth of cure. Dual-cure cements combine light and chemical curing, offering versatility for both light-accessible and deeper areas. Other types may include self-adhesive or resin-modified cements, each designed for specific applications. These variations cater to different clinical needs, enhancing treatment outcomes in dentistry.</p></p>
