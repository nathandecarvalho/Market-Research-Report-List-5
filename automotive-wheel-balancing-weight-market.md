<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Balancing Weight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel balancing weight market comprises two main types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights are designed to attach to the edge of the wheel rim, providing a secure fit and easy adjustment for precise balancing. They are commonly used in professional settings. Adhesive weights, on the other hand, stick directly to the inner rim surface. This type offers a more streamlined appearance and is ideal for alloy wheels where aesthetics are a priority. Both types ensure optimal wheel performance and safety.</p></p>
