<p><p>In the copper target market, there are primarily two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are fixed, flat surfaces that facilitate direct interaction with the copper material, commonly used in applications requiring consistent deposition and thickness. In contrast, rotating targets involve cylindrical or disc-shaped copper materials that spin during use, enhancing uniformity in coating and reducing wear. Both types serve specific industrial processes, including semiconductor manufacturing and thin-film deposition, tailoring to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
