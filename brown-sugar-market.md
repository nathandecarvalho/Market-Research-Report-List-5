<p><ul><li>Light Brown Sugar</li><li>Dark Brown Sugar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brown sugar market is primarily divided into light brown sugar and dark brown sugar. Light brown sugar contains a lower molasses content, giving it a milder flavor and a lighter color, making it ideal for baking and sweetening beverages. Conversely, dark brown sugar features a higher molasses content, resulting in a richer flavor and deeper color, which is often preferred in recipes requiring robust taste, such as gingerbread or barbecued sauces. Demand for both types varies based on culinary trends and consumer preferences.</p></p>
