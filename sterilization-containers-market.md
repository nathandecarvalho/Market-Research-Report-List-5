<p><strong>The Sterilization Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Type</li><li>Valve Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization containers market is categorized mainly into filter type and valve type containers. Filter type containers utilize specialized filters to allow steam or gas penetration while preventing contamination, ensuring effective sterilization. In contrast, valve type containers come equipped with valves that facilitate the escape of air and prevent moisture accumulation during the sterilization process. Both types are essential in maintaining sterility and efficiency in medical environments, with choices typically based on specific procedural requirements and preferences of healthcare facilities.</p></p>
