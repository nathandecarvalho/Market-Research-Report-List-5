<p><ul><li>Linear Switches</li><li>Tactile Non-Clicky Switches</li><li>Clicky Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical keyboard market features three main types of switches: linear, tactile non-clicky, and clicky switches. Linear switches provide a smooth keystroke without any tactile feedback or audible noise, appealing to gamers for rapid key presses. Tactile non-clicky switches offer a noticeable bump when actuated, enhancing typing accuracy without the noise, making them ideal for office environments. Clicky switches deliver both tactile feedback and an audible click, favored by typists seeking a satisfying typing experience. Each switch type caters to different user preferences.</p></p>
