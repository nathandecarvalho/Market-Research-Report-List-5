<p><ul><li>Cohesive Gel Implants</li><li>Highly Cohesive Gel Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gel implants market features two primary types: cohesive gel implants and highly cohesive gel implants. Cohesive gel implants consist of a softer gel that provides a natural feel, allowing for subtle movement. In contrast, highly cohesive gel implants contain a firmer gel structure that maintains its shape more effectively, reducing the risk of leakage and minimizing the potential for rippling. Both types offer distinct advantages in terms of aesthetics, safety, and patient satisfaction, catering to varying preferences and needs in breast augmentation.</p></p>
