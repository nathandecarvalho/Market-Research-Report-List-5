<p><p>The autoimmunity reagents market encompasses various diagnostic techniques, including Western Blot (BLOT), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), Indirect Immunofluorescence Assay (IFA), and Chemiluminescent Immunoassay (CLIA). BLOT is used for protein detection, while ELISA quantifies antibody levels. IFA employs fluorescence microscopy to visualize antigen-antibody interactions, and CLIA combines chemiluminescent detection with immunoassay principles. Each method offers unique advantages in sensitivity and specificity, catering to diverse clinical and research applications in the diagnosis of autoimmune disorders.</p></p>
