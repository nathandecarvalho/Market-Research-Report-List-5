<p><p>The mosquito repellent market encompasses various types of products designed to deter mosquitoes. Coils release smoke when burned, offering long-lasting protection. Vaporizers, typically electric, heat a liquid solution to disperse repellent into the air. Mats, placed in heating devices, work similarly to vaporizers but are often easier to use. Aerosols are spray-on solutions that provide immediate coverage, while creams and lotions are applied directly to the skin for personal protection. Each type caters to different preferences and environments.</p></p>
