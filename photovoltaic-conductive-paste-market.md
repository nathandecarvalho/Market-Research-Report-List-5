<p><p>The photovoltaic conductive paste market primarily consists of two types: aluminum (Al) paste and silver (Ag) paste. Al paste is predominantly used for rear contacts in solar cells, offering cost efficiency and good conductivity. In contrast, Ag paste is utilized for front-side contacts, known for its superior conductivity and light reflectivity, but at a higher cost. Together, these materials play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of solar cells in photovoltaic applications.</p></p>
