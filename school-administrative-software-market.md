<p><ul><li>Cloud-based School Administrative Software</li><li>On-premises School Administrative Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>School Administrative Software is divided into two main types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, accessed through the internet, and managed by a third-party provider. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, On-premises software is installed and maintained on the school's local servers and computers. It provides full control over data but may require a higher upfront cost and more technical expertise to manage. Each type caters to different needs and preferences within the education sector.</p></p>
