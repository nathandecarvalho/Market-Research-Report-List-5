<p><strong>The Car Fleet Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Close End Lease</li><li>Open End Lease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car fleet leasing market can be divided into two main types: closed-end leases and open-end leases. Closed-end leases allow businesses to return vehicles at the end of the lease term without additional costs, assuming mileage and condition limits are met. In contrast, open-end leases require businesses to cover any depreciation or excess mileage costs at the lease's conclusion. Both options provide flexibility for companies, but closed-end leases typically offer more predictable financial outcomes.</p></p>
