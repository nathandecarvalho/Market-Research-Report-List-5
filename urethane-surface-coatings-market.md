<p><p>The urethane surface coatings market is categorized into powder, solvent-borne, and water-borne types. Powder coatings are free of solvents and are applied electrostatically, providing durability and a smooth finish. Solvent-borne coatings contain solvents that facilitate application but can emit volatile organic compounds. Water-borne coatings, on the other hand, utilize water as a primary solvent, offering a more eco-friendly option with lower emissions. Each type serves specific applications and preferences in industries ranging from automotive to construction.</p></p>
