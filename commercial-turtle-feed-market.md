<p><strong>The Commercial Turtle Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Staple</li><li>Supplement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial turtle feed market primarily consists of two types: staple and supplement feeds. Staple feeds are designed to serve as the primary diet for turtles, providing necessary nutrients and energy for growth and maintenance. They typically come in pellets or sticks that cater to specific species or life stages. Supplement feeds, on the other hand, enhance the nutritional profile of staple diets, offering additional vitamins, minerals, and proteins. These supplements may include treats, fresh vegetables, or specialized formulated products to promote overall health.</p></p>
