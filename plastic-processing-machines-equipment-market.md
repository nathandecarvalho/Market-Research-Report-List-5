<p><p>The plastic processing machines and equipment market comprises various types designed for shaping and forming plastics. Injection molding machinery injects molten plastic into molds for precise shapes. Extruders machinery continuously pushes plastic through a die to create long shapes, like pipes or sheets. Blow molding machinery expands heated plastic into hollow forms, ideal for containers. Other equipment includes rotational molding, thermoforming, and 3D printing technologies, catering to diverse applications in industries such as packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
