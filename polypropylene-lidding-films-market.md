<p><ul><li>Single Layer Films</li><li>Dual Layer Films</li><li>Multilayer Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene lidding films market is segmented into three main types: single layer films, dual layer films, and multilayer films. Single layer films are made from a single polymer layer, providing basic sealing and barrier properties. Dual layer films combine two layers to enhance moisture and oxygen barrier capabilities, improving food preservation. Multilayer films integrate multiple polymer layers, offering superior protection against environmental factors, extending shelf life, and enabling versatile packaging solutions for various applications in the food and medical sectors.</p></p>
