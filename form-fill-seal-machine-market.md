<p><p>Form-Fill-Seal (FFS) machines are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and application. Horizontal FFS machines operate by laying the film flat, forming pouches horizontally, suitable for products like snacks and liquids. In contrast, vertical FFS machines use a vertical setup to form bags from rolled film, ideal for items such as granules and powders. Both types enhance efficiency in packaging processes by combining forming, filling, and sealing in a single operation, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
