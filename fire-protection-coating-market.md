<p><p>The fire protection coating market is primarily divided into liquid and powder fire-resistant coatings. Liquid fire-resistant coatings are versatile and easily applied to various surfaces, providing excellent adhesion and a protective barrier against flames and heat. They are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings. In contrast, powder fire-resistant coatings are applied as a dry powder and then cured through heat, creating a durable finish that resists chipping and peeling, ideal for structural steel and other surfaces requiring long-lasting fire protection.</p></p>
