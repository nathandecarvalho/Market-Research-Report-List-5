<p><p>The medical coding market comprises various systems that ensure accurate billing and documentation in healthcare. The Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System (HCPCS) categorizes services, products, and procedures, primarily focusing on Medicare and Medicaid. The International Classification of Diseases (ICD) provides standardized codes for diagnoses and health-related issues worldwide. The Current Procedural Terminology (CPT) system codes medical, surgical, and diagnostic services performed by healthcare providers. Together, these systems facilitate effective communication, billing, and data collection in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
