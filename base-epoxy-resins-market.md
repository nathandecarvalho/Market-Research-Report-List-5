<p><p>The Base Epoxy Resins market is primarily divided into two types: Glycidyl Epoxy and Non-Glycidyl Epoxy. Glycidyl Epoxy resins are derived from epichlorohydrin and bisphenol A, known for their superior mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making them suitable for coatings, adhesives, and composites. Non-Glycidyl Epoxy resins, produced from sources like phenolic compounds, are characterized by lower cytotoxicity and a more environmentally friendly profile, often used in applications where health and safety are prioritized.</p></p>
