<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Phenolic Resin</li><li>Thermosetting Phenolic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phenolic resins market is primarily segmented into thermoplastic and thermosetting types. Thermoplastic phenolic resins are characterized by their ability to be reshaped with heat, offering flexibility and ease of processing, making them suitable for applications requiring reworkability. In contrast, thermosetting phenolic resins undergo a chemical change during curing, resulting in a hardened structure that provides excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties. This makes them ideal for high-performance applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electrical insulation.</p></p>
