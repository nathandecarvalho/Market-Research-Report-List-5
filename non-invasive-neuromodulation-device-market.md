<p><p>The Non-invasive Neuromodulation Device Market includes various types designed to modify neuronal activity. Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS) uses low electrical currents for cognitive enhancement. Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation (tACS) applies alternating currents to influence brain oscillations. Transcranial Pulsed Current Stimulation (tPCS) utilizes pulse currents for targeted brain regions, while Transcranial Random Noise Stimulation (tRNS) provides random noise stimulation to enhance cognitive performance. Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT) delivers controlled seizures for severe depression treatment. Each method offers unique mechanisms for neuromodulation with therapeutic potential.</p></p>
