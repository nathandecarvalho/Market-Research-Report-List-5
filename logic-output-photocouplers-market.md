<p><p>The Logic Output Photocouplers market is segmented into three main types: AC, DC, and Logic Inverter. DC photocouplers cater to direct current signals, enabling reliable communication in low-voltage circuits. Logic Inverter photocouplers provide signal inversion for logic applications, enhancing digital circuit functionality. Each type offers distinct advantages in isolation, signal integrity, and application versatility, contributing to the growing demand across various industries.</p></p>
