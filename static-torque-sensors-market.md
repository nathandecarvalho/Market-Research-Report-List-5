<p><p>The static torque sensors market is primarily divided into two types: rotary torque sensors and reaction torque sensors. Rotary torque sensors measure torque in rotating applications, typically featuring a rotating element that delivers precise readings during dynamic conditions. In contrast, reaction torque sensors are designed for stationary systems, measuring torque exerted on a stationary object, making them ideal for applications like assembly and testing. Both types are crucial in various industries, ensuring accuracy and reliability in torque measurement and analysis.</p></p>
