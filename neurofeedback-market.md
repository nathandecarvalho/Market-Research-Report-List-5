<p><p>The neurofeedback market encompasses various modalities, each targeting specific brain functions. Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF) focuses on training brain rhythms to enhance self-regulation. Low-Energy Neurofeedback System (LENS) operates by detecting and modulating subtle brainwave patterns for therapeutic effects. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) Neurofeedback measures blood flow and oxygenation in the brain, promoting better mental performance. Additionally, other market categories include various emerging technologies and approaches that leverage brainwave monitoring to improve cognitive and emotional health.</p></p>
