<p><p>Automotive AHSS (Advanced High-Strength Steel) market includes various types such as Dual Phase Steel, Martensitic Steel, Boron Steel, and TRIP Steel. Dual Phase Steel offers a combination of high strength and elongation, making it suitable for structural parts. Martensitic Steel is known for its high strength and hardness, ideal for components requiring wear resistance. Boron Steel is used for crash-resistant parts due to its high tensile strength. TRIP Steel provides a good balance of strength, ductility, and formability, making it suitable for automotive applications.</p></p>
