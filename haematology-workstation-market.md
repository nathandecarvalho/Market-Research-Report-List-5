<p><p>The hematology workstation market is primarily categorized into two types: automatic and semiautomatic hematology workstations. Automatic hematology workstations are fully automated systems designed for high-throughput testing, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in blood analysis. These systems require minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large laboratories. In contrast, semiautomatic hematology workstations combine manual and automated processes, providing flexibility for smaller labs that may not need high-volume testing. These workstations offer a balance between user control and operational efficiency.</p></p>
