There are four main types of rubber additives: Rubber Antioxidants which prevent the rubber from oxidation and deterioration, Rubber Accelerators which help speed up the vulcanization process, Insoluble Sulfur which provides better heat resistance and flexibility, and Other additives such as fillers, plasticizers, and colorants. Each type of additive plays a specific role in enhancing the properties of the rubber product.</p></p>
