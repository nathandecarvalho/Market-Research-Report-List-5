<p><ul><li>High Temperature Austenite Phase</li><li>Low Temperature Martensite Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fe-based shape memory alloys market is divided into two primary types based on temperature phases: high-temperature austenite phase and low-temperature martensite phase. The high-temperature austenite phase is characterized by its ability to retain shape recovery at elevated temperatures, making it suitable for applications in industries like aerospace and automotive. Conversely, the low-temperature martensite phase exhibits shape memory effects at lower temperatures, ideal for medical devices and consumer products. Each phase caters to specific applications based on thermal responsiveness and mechanical properties.</p></p>
